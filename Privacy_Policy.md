Welcome to Stamps. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, username, and profile details (such as your full name and bio).
User Content: We collect the photos you capture and share (your "Stamps") along with the captions you provide.
Interactions: We store information regarding your likes, comments, and the users you follow/unfollow to provide a social experience.
Usage Data: We may collect technical logs and app performance data (e.g., diagnostics) to improve your experience.
2. How We Use Your Information

To provide and maintain the core features of Stamps (e.g., daily sharing).
To facilitate social interactions (likes, follows, and notifications).
To ensure account security and perform technical troubleshooting.
To analyze general usage patterns to improve app performance.
3. Data Storage and Third-Party Services Stamps uses third-party services like Supabase for secure cloud storage and authentication. Your data is stored on encrypted servers. We do not sell your personal data to any third parties for marketing purposes.

4. User Rights You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. You can edit your profile or delete your shared "Stamps" directly through the application. For account deletion requests, please contact us.

5. Age Requirement Stamps is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

6. Changes to This Policy We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of the app after any changes signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.
